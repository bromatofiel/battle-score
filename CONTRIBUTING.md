# Contributing to BattleScore

Thank you for your interest in contributing to BattleScore!

## Contribution Terms

By submitting a pull request or contribution, you agree that:

- You are the original author of the contribution;
- You grant BattleScore a perpetual, worldwide, royalty-free right
  to use, modify, relicense, and distribute your contribution
  as part of the BattleScore project;
- Your contribution does not impose any additional license restrictions.

This allows BattleScore to continue offering the software under
the Business Source License and commercial licenses.

If you do not agree with these terms, please do not submit a contribution.
